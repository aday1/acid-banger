About this toy

The music you hear is generated in your browser by a randomised algorithm, below you can see the notes and parameters that are currently in use. You can also interact with various parameters and buttons manually. The green autopilot switches change how automatic playback is. Leave them on for a lean-back experience. Buttons labelled with a circular arrow will generate new patterns.

The Endless Acid Banger was created by Vitling. If you want to support my work, please consider buying my music or sponsoring my GitHub.

This build is an aday fork of the original Vitling acid toy. Added in this fork: MIDI clock sync + slave mode, MIDI learn and mapping helpers, OSC bridge support, transport helpers, and expanded performance UI wiring.
